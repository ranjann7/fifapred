Engine

Simulation Algorithm

1. Group Stage Prediction

AI predicts winners of each group by analyzing team strengths and head-to-head records.

2. Knockout Simulation

For each knockout match, ML model calculates win probability and predicts progression.

3. Monte Carlo Analysis

Runs 10,000 simulations to determine championship probability for each team.

4. Champion Selection

Final prediction based on highest champion probability with confidence level.
