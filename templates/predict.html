How It Works

1. Team Data Analysis: The AI analyzes FIFA rankings, historical performance, attack/defense ratings

2. Feature Engineering: Creates meaningful features from team statistics

3. ML Model Prediction: Uses a trained Random Forest model on historical international results

4. Probability Calculation: Computes win probabilities for both teams and draws

5. Score Prediction: Predicts likely final scoreline based on team strengths
